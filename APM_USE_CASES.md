- **Social Media Sanity Check**: Scan X rants, TikTok "hacks," or Reddit threads for foreign interference, botfly myths, or hype traps. Scores 1-5 on purity; auto-flags shares with receipts to dodge doom-scroll regret and arm viral truths.
- **Health & Wellness Vetting**: Audit supplement claims, aging hacks, or viral remedies (e.g., "This shot reverses telomeres?") against lab trials, peer studies, and FDA shadows. Personalizes thriving plans, cutting worry over snake oil.
- **Personal Finance Fortress**: Verify "get rich quick" schemes, crypto whispers, or budget tips before committing cash. Eternal layers cross-check market history and scam patterns, dodging rug-pulls for real alpha stacks.
- **News Consumption Filter** (New!): Sift daily headlines or podcast bites for bias creeps or agenda spins—balanced verdicts from diverse sources, turning info diets into nutrient-dense truth feasts.
